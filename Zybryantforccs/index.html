Zy's Bio
  • Zyahna Bryant is a community advocate, lifelong Charlottesville resident, and proud alum of Charlottesville City Schools.
  • Education background (Trailblazer Elementary → CHS → UVA)
  • Current MA student in History at UVA
  • Inspired by family legacy (Great-niece of Charlottesville 12 members Marvin & William Townsend)
  • Track record of advocacy in education equity
  • Leadership & Experience Highlights
    • Founder of the CHS Black Student Union
    • Former Student School Board Representative
    • Appointed by City Council to the Charlottesville Youth Council
    • Appointed by Gov. Northam to the Virginia African American Advisory Board (youngest member)

Platform & Priorities

Student Engagement & Learning

  • Encouraging students to take an active role in their education
  • Implementing hands-on, interactive, and experiential learning approaches

Empowering Youth Voice & Representation

  • Elevating student perspectives in school decision-making
  • Strengthening communication between students, teachers, and leadership

Equitable & Empowering Schools for All

  • Addressing racial and socioeconomic disparities in education
  • Ensuring all students have access to quality resources

Improving Literacy Rates

  • Investing in early childhood reading programs
  • Expanding literacy support for students struggling in reading and writing

Supporting Educators

  • Providing teachers with the resources and funding they need to succeed
  • Advocating for fair wages and professional development opportunities

Student Success Meetings & Mental Health Support

  • Refining student success processes to better support families
  • Expanding access to mental health services and wellness programs in schools

Retention & Addressing Chronic Absenteeism

  • Implementing targeted interventions to keep students engaged in school
  • Strengthening family and community involvement in student success
